Who We Are
About VGA Holdings

VGA Holdings was established in 2014 in the Kingdom of Bahrain and operates across a diverse range of sectors including Brokerage, Consultancy, Chemical Distribution, Oil & Gas Pipeline Services, and strategic investment portfolios.

We believe that goal setting is the foundation of sustainable success. With deep industry expertise and global market knowledge, VGA partners with clients to transform vision into measurable outcomes.

Our approach is practical, data-driven, and grounded in long-term value creation.
